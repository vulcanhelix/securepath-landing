# Security Posture Assessment Service Framework

Security Posture Assessment Service Framework
Empowering Your Organization’s Cyber Resilience

Overview
Our Security Posture Assessment Service is designed to evaluate your organization’s current cybersecurity defences, identify vulnerabilities, and provide a strategic roadmap for risk mitigation and compliance enhancement. This framework helps you understand your security strengths and gaps while aligning with industry best practices and regulatory requirements.

Objectives

Evaluate Current Security Posture: Conduct a comprehensive review of policies, controls, and infrastructure.

Identify Vulnerabilities & Risks: Use industry-leading tools and methodologies (e.g., NIST CSF, CIS Controls) to uncover security gaps.

Develop a Remediation Roadmap: Prioritize risk areas and outline actionable steps to bolster defences.

Enhance Regulatory Compliance: Ensure your security controls align with standards like GDPR, PDPL, POPIA, PCI DSS, DORA, and more.

Our Methodology

Pre-Assessment & Data Collection:
• Gather existing security documentation, network diagrams, and policies.
• Conduct interviews with key stakeholders.

Assessment Phase:
• Perform vulnerability scans and risk analysis.
• Evaluate technical controls (e.g., network security, endpoint protection, IAM, encryption).
• Review incident response procedures and security awareness programs.

Reporting & Recommendations:
• Deliver a detailed report including a risk matrix, gap analysis, and prioritized remediation plan.
• Present clear recommendations to improve your overall security posture.

Continuous Improvement:
• Offer periodic re-assessments and monitoring to track progress and adjust strategies as new threats emerge.

Compliance and Regulatory Alignment Recommendations
