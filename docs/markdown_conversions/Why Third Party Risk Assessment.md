# Why Third Party Risk Assessment

Why the need for Third Party Risks assessment?

As companies embrace new technologies to drive their digital transformation initiatives, cyber risks remain a top priority concern for business leaders. As a consequence of wide-reaching and successful cyberattacks, Third Party Management (TPM) has become companies’ focal point. To maintain a sound security posture, organizations need to assess the vendor ecosystem to ensure that risks of cybersecurity, privacy breach, ethics and compliance are mitigated and environmental, social, and governance goals are achieved.

Our consultants, with years of expertise, are renowned for conducting and evaluating third-party security assessments on behalf of our esteemed clients. We pride ourselves on our methodology in evaluating third parties’ security controls, leveraging our deep understanding and unparalleled skill set to ensure that third parties’ systems and processes are robust, compliant, and secure.

Our Approach

How does it work?

Third-party risk management is a process of holding enterprises accountable to good risk management practices.

Improve the risk management capabilities of your third parties to improve the security of the Internet.

Identify and centrally manage all vendors in a single platform. Gaining visibility into your third-party ecosystem is the first and most important step to understanding your risk exposure.

Extend that visibility to third-party technology usage. When a breach that impacts a specific technology occurs, you can map impacted vendors easily and target risk assessments accordingly.

Automate risk assessments, including questionnaire selection, response workflow and reporting.

Score responses based on specific risk thresholds. This will enable your team to target the most critical risks first.

Offer proactive reports on incident response progress and mitigations to satisfy the needs of regulators, board members, and other stakeholders.
